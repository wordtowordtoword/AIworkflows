# Zapier + GPT Email Summary Workflow

**Use Case:** Auto-summarize incoming emails and store summaries in Google Sheets.

**Workflow:**
1. Gmail trigger: New email received
2. Zapier sends email body to OpenAI (GPT-4)
3. GPT summarizes the content
4. Zapier appends summary to Google Sheet

**Tools:**
- Gmail (Trigger)
- OpenAI (Action)
- Google Sheets (Action)

**Business Value:**  
Speeds up email triage, enables faster executive review.
